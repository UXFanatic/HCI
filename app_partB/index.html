1.0. Realistic Lawn Care

February, 26, 2015
by Andres Burden

Realistic Lawn Care is a business dedicated to enhancing commercial and residential properties. They provide year round services for businesses and residences: landscaping design, lawn maintenance, bed maintenance, lawn fertilization, shrub trimming, pine straw, seasonal flowers and plantings.

This project consists of a highly responsive web application for Realistic Lawn Care (RLC). The information outlined in this document will summarize the application architecture, conceptual design, and execution plans.

2.0. Desired Features

The features, listed below, have been gathered by observing, studying, and surveying business members. These are deemed important to the business online presence. In addition, direct feedback from the business’ clientele has been taken into consideration in the development of this project. This feedback has been collected from RLC’s records.

3.0. Conceptual Design

The conceptual design for this project focuses on the hypothetical functionality for Realistic Lawn Care’s v2.0.0. website. The visual representation documented below will potentially be used in a final product.

With the constant increase of Internet usage and traffic, RLC’s website should follow today’s web standards outlines by the W3School as well as many other reputable organizations.

The main goal for this project consists of implementing a very accessible website with a high degree of adaptiveness and accessibility. In order to achieve this goal, the conceptual design will emphasize on key components: global navigation, footer, and page layout.

The hierarchical structure of the project is shown below. In this structure, the order of the content is relative to its relevancy with the objective of the site.
