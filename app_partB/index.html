1.0. Realistic Lawn Care

February, 26, 2015
by Andres Burden

Realistic Lawn Care is a business dedicated to enhancing commercial and residential properties. They provide year round services for businesses and residences: landscaping design, lawn maintenance, bed maintenance, lawn fertilization, shrub trimming, pine straw, seasonal flowers and plantings.

Realistic Lawn Care main objective is to enhance the outdoor living environment of their customers by providing superior, top quality, professional, and reliable property maintenance at competitive prices.

Main Services

Realistic Lawn & Care services residential and commercial properties in the northeast and metro areas of Atlanta. While most of their services are very hyper-local, they are not limited to these specific regions. Some of these services include:

  • Laying Sod/Seeding Lawns
  • Planting Trees/Shrubbery
  • Clean-Up
  • Plating Flower Beds
  • Bush Trimming
  • Fertilizing

The company’s client base is comprised of 67% residential and 33% commercial. It is important to highlight that some of the most popular services are as follows:

  • 19%Landscape Design
  • 12%Landscape Installation
  • 15%Lawn Maintenance
  • 11%Custom Jobs

Percentage represent the net amount of services performed by Realistic Lawn Care. Data collected Jan 2014 - March 2014. This does not include partners.

Project Summary

This project aims to create a new online presentation for Realistic Lawn Care with a modern and adaptive interface. The website shall inform visitor about the business and the services it offers as well as specials and promotions. In addition, it should provide a way for visitors to engage with the business with contact information and social engagement.

Project Objective

The main objective of this website is to engage online visitors with the business by increasing the lead conversion rates. This website shall be accessible via any current device, especially mobile.

Feedback

Ryan Chaskin, owner and founder, shared some feedback and insight regarding the project. Ryan was involved when the original website was developed and launched. He has also been instrumental in providing insight in some of the main components of the business.

Some of the feedback is as follows:

  • Quoting – Clients are frequently calling to inquire about the quoting process. This should be stated online. It is free for all clients.
  • Customers are always concern with pricing. Realistic Lawn Care does not have a fixed rate. The business believes in providing the best value to all customers. Hence, all jobs are quoted.
  • Annual Contracts – Customers are always interested in this information. The year-round services should be featured online.
  • Payment – Customers are frequently inquiring about payment methods. It would save the business time by displaying this information online (very accessible within the page).

Business Requirements

These requirements are deemed critical for Realistic Lawn Care in order to meet the organizational objective(s).

Computer Literacy

(High) Web users should know how to use the site and browser information.

Language Requirement

The site will operate in English only. Therefore, knowledge of the English language is required.

Note: The site's content should be organized in a manner that it communicates services and pricing to those who do not have good knowledge of the English language.

Cell Phone Requirement

Users are required to have a cell phone, or equivalent service, in order to conduct business with Realistic Lawn Care.

Email Requirement

A must) Users must have access to an email service in order to efficiently conduct business. Contracts, invoices, and inquiries are managed predominantly via email.
