Realistic Lawn Care

v2.0.0.
by Andres Burden

Realistic Lawn Care is a business dedicated to enhancing commercial and residential properties. They provide year round services for businesses and residences: landscaping design, lawn maintenance, bed maintenance, lawn fertilization, shrub trimming, pine straw, seasonal flowers and plantings.

Realistic Lawn Care main objective is to enhance the outdoor living environment of their customers by providing superior, top quality, professional, and reliable property maintenance at competitive prices.

Main Services

Realistic Lawn & Care services residential and commercial clients in the northeast and metro areas of Atlanta for very affordable prices. While most of their services are very hyper-local, they are not limited to these specific regions. Some of these services include:

  • Laying Sod/Seeding Lawns
  • Planting Trees/Shrubbery
  • Clean-Up
  • Plating Flower Beds
  • Bush Trimming
  • Fertilizing

Project Summary

This project aims to create a new online presentation for Realistic Lawn Care with a new, modern, and responsive interface. The website shall inform visitor about the business and the services it offers. In addition, it should provide a way for visitors to engage with the business with contact information and social engagement.

Project Objective

The main objective of this website is to engage online visitors with the business by increasing the lead conversion rates. This website shall be accessible via any current device, especially mobile.

Tasks and Actions

This section documents the numerous tasks and actions the user community will be able to perform with the launching of the v2.0.0. site.

Learn about the business

Users shall have the ability to learn about who Realistic Lawn Care is and the services it offers. When accessing www.realisticlawncare.com users will be directed to a home page that features the business as a whole. Additional content will be featured to give users the ability to learn more. User may click on the “Contact” page to inquiry for additional information.

Learn about services and estimation

Users will have the opportunity to learn about the various services offered by Realistic Lawn Care. On click, users should be directed to a page that features the services along with the quoting and estimation process. User shall learn about the methods of payment.

View previous jobs

Users will have the ability to view previous jobs completed by the business. A photo gallery will be featured to showcase pictures that are relevant to the task. The photo gallery will be highly accessible.
